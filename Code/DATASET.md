## Region selection

17 mainland regions (MK01–MK19 excluding **MK16 Central Ostrobothnia**
and **MK21 Åland**). MK16 dropped because it is the smallest mainland
region (~68k population, ~9k housing-indebted households), which
weakens PD estimates without adding geographic diversity. MK21 is
excluded as it is an autonomous region with materially different
mortgage-market characteristics.

| `n_indebted` (informational) | 8 847 – 260 068 | **StatFin 157w**, Housing loan debts, 2024, "Number of indebted household-dwelling units" (pxdata.stat.fi StatFin/velk/157w). |
| `mean_debt` (informational) | €73 037 – €158 329 | **StatFin 157w**, Housing loan debts, 2024, "Mean of debt" (same table). |
| `mean_interest` (informational) | €2 921 – €6 079 | **StatFin 157w**, Housing loan debts, 2024, "Mean of interest" (same table). |
| `share_indebted_pct` (informational) | 23.0 % – 30.9 % | **StatFin 157w**, Housing loan debts, 2024, "Share of indebted household-dwelling units (%)" (same table). |
| `p_zeros[k]` | 0.0103 – 0.0181 | **Approximated** (no public regional PD for Finnish mortgages). National baseline `PD_BASE = 0.015` (1.5 %) is the residential-mortgage NPL band reported in the **EBA Risk Dashboard** for Finland (2022–2024: 0.5–1.5 %) and consistent with the bank-reported PDs in the **FIN-FSA Disclosure requirement for mortgage credit bank operations** report. Regionalised with a Vasicek-style pass-through from regional unemployment: `pd[r] = 0.015 × (1 + 0.6 × (u[r] − ū)/ū)`, where `u[r]` is the 2024 regional unemployment rate from **StatFin 13al** (Labour Force Survey, "Unemployment rate, %") and `ū` is its 17-region mean. Clipped to [0.05 %, 10 %]. |
| `rhos[k]` | 0.09 (constant) | **QSVT4CRA paper default** (cell 2 of `CRA_QSVT.ipynb`). |
| `lgd[k]` (€) | €1.31 × 10⁸ – €8.24 × 10⁹ | **Computed** = `mean_debt[r] × n_indebted[r] × LGD_RATE`, where `LGD_RATE = 0.20` is the residential-mortgage LGD used in the **EBA EU-wide Stress Test** (2023 baseline). FIN-FSA mortgage-credit bank disclosures report realised LGDs in the 10–25 % band for Finnish residential mortgages with full-recourse lending. Sum of `lgd` over regions ≈ **€18.4 bn** (upper bound of portfolio loss in the worst-case scenario where every region defaults). |
| `F_values[k]` (2 factors) | max row norm = 0.30 | **Computed**. Two latent factors: factor 1 = standardised regional unemployment (`StatFin 13al`), factor 2 = standardised regional mean debt (`StatFin 157w`). Both z-scores are scaled by a common factor so `√(F₁² + F₂²) ≤ 0.30` for every region — preserves the GCI conditional-PD well-definedness (the GCI model needs `F² < 1` per row; we keep margin). |
| `n_z` | 2 | **QSVT4CRA paper default** (qubits per Gaussian factor). |
| `z_max` | 2 | **QSVT4CRA paper default** (truncation of the standard normal). |

## Caveats

1. **PDs are approximated, not measured.** No public source provides
   region-level mortgage PDs in Finland. The 1.5 % national baseline
   comes from EU-wide EBA reporting; the regionalisation via
   unemployment is a standard Basel/IFRS9-style sensitivity adjustment.
   If the user later obtains bank-estimated PDs (e.g. from the
   FIN-FSA disclosure report or a partner bank's internal rating
   system), they should override `p_zeros` in the notebook directly.

2. **LGDs use a single rate (20 %).** The 157w table does not contain
   LGDs. The chosen 20 % is the EBA stress-test residential
   mortgage baseline. If a finer view is needed, FIN-FSA mortgage
   disclosures provide bank-estimated LGDs.

3. **F_values are standardised, not estimated correlations.** The
   2-factor loadings are scaled z-scores, not covariance-matrix
   entries. This satisfies the model constraint (`F² < 1`) and gives
   a meaningful spread (high-unemployment and high-debt regions
   load positively on both factors). For a strict correlation-matrix
   approach, a regional mortgage-default correlation matrix would
   need to be estimated from loan-level bank data, which is not
   publicly available.

4. **K = 17 implies 2¹⁷ = 131 072 scenarios.** The classical
   bisection search in cell 13 enumerates all of them; expect longer
   runtime than the K = 4 baseline (paper). For K > 20, prefer the
   `MultivariateGCI_Poly` truncation in `Code/multivariateGCI.py`.

5. **Kainuu (MK18) unemployment** is "." (not reported by Statistics
   Finland in 2024) and has been replaced by the 17-region mean
   (8.16 %).
